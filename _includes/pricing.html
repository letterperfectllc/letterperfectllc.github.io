Service Prices

Complimentary Services

I will schedule a complimentary 30-minute meeting, in person or by phone, for us to discuss your project and how we can work together to achieve your goals.

I will also provide a copy edit or developmental edit of the first five pages of your manuscript. This will help to provide a reasonable estimate of the time and cost for working on the project, as well as allowing you to determine if I am a good fit for your needs.

Pricing

All services are charged at a simple rate of $40 per hour.

Billing Options

Monthly

Billing is completed on the final day of each month for any work done within that month. Payment is due within 14 days of receipt of the invoice unless otherwise negotiated.

Installments

Payments may be spread out over time to help meet your needs. Feel free to propose an approach that works for your budget. After we agree on a payment schedule, I will present a contract for both of us to sign.

Monetary Cap

Set a dollar limit for your project, and I will do as much work as possible within your budget.
